Where do I fit?

“Where do I fit” is an interactive experience created by a team of IxD graduate students at Cal State East Bay in an attempt to gather data on Art students interests in different majors.

The Story

In Fall 2018, California State University East Bay academic system converted from quarters to semesters. Along with this change, the Art department revised and added several degrees and courses that did not exist before then. This gave the students a chance to extend or change their degree. Since we are through the first few months of this change, the department decided that we need to know more from the students to be able to adjust the courses and classes to their demand and to make sure that students are in the correct path to their desired degree.
